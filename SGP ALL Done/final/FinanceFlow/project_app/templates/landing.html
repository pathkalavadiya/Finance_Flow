Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about security, pricing, and migration.

Is my data secure?

Yes — we use industry-standard 256-bit encryption, secure hosting, and optional two-factor authentication to protect your financial data.

Can I import my bank statements?

You can import CSV files and map columns during the import flow to bring historical data into FinanceFlow. We support most bank formats.

Is there a mobile app?

Our web app is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers. Native iOS and Android apps are coming soon!

Do you support multiple currencies?

Yes — multi-currency is available in Pro and Team plans with automatic or manual rate settings for accurate tracking.

Can I use this for business accounting?

FinanceFlow is ideal for small businesses and freelancers. Export-ready reports make accountant handoffs simple.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. No questions asked, and you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
